Base rules

The rules are what you can convince your opponent to accept. However, we recommend:

  1. All pieces are Horse (King is Horse but acts as a King piece)

Variations

  1. Act of god: third parties may intervene, however they should try to be fair to both players.
  2. Each piece retains its original move set but has an L shape.
  3. Each player brings their own horses (like Warhammer)
  4. Horses must kiss before winning
